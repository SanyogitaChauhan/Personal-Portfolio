Project Overview

The Fees Management System is a comprehensive Java application developed in NetBeans IDE to manage student fee records efficiently for educational institutions such as schools, colleges, and coding institutes. This project aims to streamline the entire fee management process, including fee collection, tracking, report generation, and maintaining records ,printing Receipt in a secure and user-friendly manner.

Can Add, update, or delete student and course details and link students with specific courses for accurate fee calculations. Automated calculation of fees including GST. Generate fee receipts for paid fees and allow printing or saving as PDF. Export reports in various formats such as Excel or PDF. All Student and course details can be viewed
